F.A.Q.

  • What is Bella Onojie?

    Bella Onojie is a convenient food delivery app that connects users with a variety of restaurants and eateries in their area. With just a few taps, you can order your favorite meals and have them delivered straight to your doorstep.

  • How does Bella Onojie work?

    Using Bella Onojie is simple! First, download the app from the App Store or Google Play Store. Then, create an account, browse through the list of available restaurants, select your desired items, and proceed to checkout. Once your order is confirmed, our delivery partners will ensure your food is delivered promptly.

  • What areas does Bella Onojie serve?

    Bella Onojie operates internationally! You can check if we deliver to your location by entering your address in the app.

  • What type of cuisine does Bella Onojie offer?

    Bella Onojie partners with a diverse range of restaurants, offering cuisines to suit every palate. From local delicacies to international favorites, you'll find an extensive selection of dishes to choose from.

  • Can I schedule a delivery in advance?

    Yes, Bella Onojie allows you to schedule deliveries in advance. Simply select the desired delivery time during the checkout process, and we'll ensure your order arrives at your preferred time.

  • Are there any delivery fees?

    Delivery fees may vary depending on your location and the restaurant you order from. You'll be able to view any applicable fees before placing your order.

  • How can I pay for my order?

    Bella Onojie accepts various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, mobile wallets (cryptocurrency), cash on delivery, and trading for family members. Choose the option that's most convenient for you during checkout.

  • Is there a minimum order requirement?

    Minimum order requirements may vary depending on the restaurant you choose. You'll be notified of any minimum order amounts before completing your purchase.

  • What if there's an issue with my order?

    If you encounter any problems with your order, such as missing items or incorrect items, please contact our customer support team immediately. We'll work to resolve the issue and ensure you receive the best possible service.

  • How can I provide feedback about my experience?

    Your feedback is important to us! You can leave a review directly within the app after your order is delivered. Additionally, you can reach out to our customer support team with any suggestions or concerns you may have. We're always striving to improve our service based on your feedback.
